## AI Discovery Engine — App Concept

AI Discovery Engine is an AI-native SaaS platform that transforms fragmented internet research into structured, reusable and actionable business intelligence datasets. Users can type natural language prompts such as “Find 200 AI app studios in Europe open to startup partnerships” and the system automatically performs discovery, scraping, crawling, enrichment, AI tagging, deduplication, semantic classification and persistent storage.

The platform is designed for founders, agencies, sales teams, recruiters, investors, consultants and enterprise teams who currently lose hours switching between Google, LinkedIn, directories, Perplexity, Crunchbase, Apollo, spreadsheets and manual copy-paste workflows.

Unlike chatbots that only generate temporary answers, AI Discovery Engine creates reusable operational datasets users can filter, monitor, enrich, export, share and query through APIs.

Core workflows:
- AI prompt search
- multi-source discovery
- scraping and crawling
- company enrichment
- AI tagging and scoring
- faceted filtering
- saved lists
- recurring monitoring
- exports
- API access
- admin monitoring
